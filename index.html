Rose Symbolism

Roses have been associated with love and beauty for centuries. Different colors of roses also have different meanings, such as red for love, yellow for friendship, and white for purity.

Largest Flower

The Rafflesia arnoldii holds the record for being the world's largest flower. It can grow up to three feet in diameter and has a distinctive odor that resembles rotting flesh to attract pollinators.

Flower Language

Floriography is the language of flowers, where different flowers and their colors convey specific messages and emotions. It was a popular form of communication during the Victorian era.
